coraldefense

Coraldefense is Emily Mo, an experimental pop musician based in the Boston area.

In her 2022 album Housefly Sting, Coraldefense blends glitchy electronics with silky, shoegaze-inspired synth textures, pulling influences from artists like Broadcast, Sweet Trip, and Sheena Ringo. Housefly Sting lyrically explores the chaotic ripple effect of human interactions and the ironic emptiness of the infinitely resourceful internet.

"Hypnogogic shapes emerge in the hypnotic leads and shadowy chord progressions giving housefly sting a sense of timelessness and wonder. There are so many interesting ideas here and the narrative sequence heightens their impact." - Foxy Digitalis
