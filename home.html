coraldefense

Coraldefense is Emily Mo, an experimental pop musician based in the Boston area. In her 2022 album Housefly Sting ITALICIZE, Coraldefense blends glitchy electronics with organic, shoegaze-inspired synth textures. HOUSEFLY STING ITALICIZE lyrically explores the chaotic ripple effect of human interactions and the ironic emptiness of the infinitely resourceful internet.
